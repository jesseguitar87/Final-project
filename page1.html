Royce Gracie

Royce Gracie is a practitioner of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and son of Helio Gracie, the inventor of Jiu Jitsu. He had a career in mixed martial arts and was present in the early days of the UFC, with the highlight of his career being his victory in an 8 man tournament against several men much larger and stronger than he was.

Achievements

  • Won the championship at UFC 1, 2 and 4.
  • Was inaugurated into the UFC hall of fame in 2003.
  • Most bouts won in tournaments in UFC history (11)
  • Most fights in a single night in UFC history (4)
  • Longest fight in UFC history (36 minutes)
  • Black Belt Magazine Competitor of the Year (1994)
